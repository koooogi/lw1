At Tokyo Magic College, not all problems are solved by explosions of the cursed
energy. Part of the work is done by analysts - magicians who study the consequences
missions, compare techniques and evaluate the effectiveness of operations.

One of these analysts works outside the central archive. He doesn't have a server room
system, no automatic report generation and no access to internal
college infrastructure. All he has is a folder with mission files,
sent by different units. 

Files come in different formats. Sometimes it's simple text, sometimes
structured JSON, sometimes XML. They all describe completed missions, but
look different. The analyst needs to quickly open the file, understand what happened,
and get a mission summary.
